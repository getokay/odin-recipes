Pizza

A great alternative to your regular pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pre-baked pizza crust
  • Cup of pesto
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Olives
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 230 degrees Celcius
  2. Spread pesto on pizza crust.Top with tomatoes, onions, olives and cheese
  3. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned
